Best Chicken in Town!

Finger-licken' Chicken was started in 1847 by a prospector named Dwight Licken. He found the perfect combination of herbs, spices and minerals to create the ultimate fried chicken treat.

History

After Dwight Licken started servin' chicken to other gold miners, he decided to expand his operation and opened up a food truck (A resturant on the back of a team of oxen). To this day, no one knows why he thought this was a good idea and due to the lawsuits, Finger-licken Chicken is legally unable to open a single new store outside of the San-Fernando Valley
